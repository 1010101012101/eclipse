Overall Goals

This section lists the goals for Eclipse 3.6 for the Platform and JDT Debug components as listed on the Eclipse Project 3.6 Draft Plan.

[1] Performance. Monitor overall performance and memory consumption.

[2] Reliability. Provide APIs for clients where needed and fix critical bugs.

[3] Keep Eclipse vibrant and attractive. Investigate enhancements such as debugging without the debug view and support for multi-context debugging.
