IWorkspaceRunnable and IWorkspace.run

Users of the 2.1 method IWorkspace.run(IWorkspaceRunnable, IProgressMonitor) should revisit their uses of this method and consider using the richer method IWorkspace.run(IWorkspaceRunnable, ISchedulingRule, IProgressMonitor).

Motivation

The old IWorkspace.run method acquires a lock on the entire workspace for the duration of the IWorkspaceRunnable.

Go back to concurrency home.
