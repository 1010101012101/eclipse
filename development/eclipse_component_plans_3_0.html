Eclipse 3.0
Component Milestone Plans

The Eclipse 3.0 plan lays out a series of milestones along the way to the 3.0 release. These milestones are at roughly 6 week intervals. Each of the Eclipse component teams maintains a component-level plan detailing what they'll be working on for the upcoming milestone.
